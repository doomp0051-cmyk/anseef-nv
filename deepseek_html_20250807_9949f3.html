Packaging Design

EcoPack System

A sustainable packaging solution for the cosmetics industry that reduces plastic waste by 82% while maintaining premium quality standards.

Client: EcoBeauty
2023
Sustainability, Packaging, 3D Modeling

The Challenge

The beauty industry generates billions of plastic packaging units annually, most of which end up in landfills. EcoBeauty approached us to develop sustainable packaging for their new skincare line that would maintain premium appeal while being environmentally responsible.

Market research showed that while 78% of consumers wanted eco-friendly options, they wouldn't compromise on product protection or luxury feel. Our challenge was to create packaging that met all these criteria while being cost-effective to produce at scale.

Research & Development

We conducted extensive material testing with various biodegradable options, including molded pulp, mycelium, and plant-based polymers. Each material was evaluated for:

  • Protective qualities during shipping
  • Moisture resistance
  • Shelf appeal
  • Manufacturing scalability
  • End-of-life disposal options

The Solution

Our final design uses a combination of molded pulp for structural elements and a thin, plant-based biodegradable film for moisture protection. The textured surface provides a premium tactile experience while clearly communicating its sustainable credentials through subtle embossing and earthy color tones.

Key innovations included:

  • Interlocking design that eliminates need for plastic inserts
  • Water-based coatings for moisture resistance
  • Minimalist branding that works with the natural material texture
  • Compostable adhesive solutions

Results

The packaging reduced plastic use by 82% while maintaining product integrity through rigorous testing. Customer surveys showed:

  • 45% increase in purchase intent compared to conventional packaging
  • 92% recognition of sustainable qualities without explicit labeling
  • 88% satisfaction with premium feel and presentation

The success of this project led to adoption across EcoBeauty's entire product line and has since been licensed to three other beauty brands looking to improve their sustainability credentials.
